About Us

We Are Pioneers in Scientific Innovation

Niharika Scientific Centre is the first and leading scientific store in Janakpur, dedicated to providing high-quality laboratory materials, chemicals, and educational accessories. Since its establishment in 2062 B.S. (2005 A.D.), the center has become a trusted partner for educational institutions, medical laboratories, and research facilities seeking reliable and authentic scientific supplies.

We take pride in offering a diverse range of products that cater to various scientific disciplines. Our inventory includes chemicals of different grades, suitable for academic, research, and professional use. In addition, we supply a wide selection of laboratory equipment across multiple faculties, including Mathematics, Agriculture, Pathology, and more. Whether it's a school science lab needing basic tools or an advanced medical facility requiring specialized apparatus, we ensure the timely delivery of top-quality materials to meet every unique demand.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction sets us apart. We work closely with institutions of all levels, from general schools to engineering and medical colleges, providing materials that meet industry standards. Our mission is to support scientific education and research by delivering the best products and services possible. With years of expertise and a strong focus on reliability, Niharika Scientific Centre continues to be a cornerstone in the region’s scientific community.

Our Sister Organization

Neha Sangit Bigyan Kendra

Neha Sangeet Bigyan Kendra is proudly situated in the heart of Mithila, in Janakpur, and was established in 2062 B.S. (2005 A.D.). Our primary mission is to serve educational institutions by supplying high-quality scientific and musical equipment. The journey of our inception began when various schools expressed increasing demands during educational workshops, highlighting the need for a reliable and dedicated supplier in the region. Recognizing this opportunity, we stepped forward to fulfill these requirements with a commitment to quality and service excellence.

Since our establishment, we have earned the trust and satisfaction of numerous educational institutions, including schools, colleges, and even prominent engineering and medical colleges across districts like Dhanusha, Mahottari, Siraha, Sindhuli, Sarlahi, Saptari, Bara, and some hilly regions. We take pride in providing scientific equipment at reasonable prices, ensuring accessibility without compromising on quality. To simplify the ordering process, we also offer detailed quotations to help institutions select their required items with clarity and ease. Our goal is to continue fostering long-term relationships through exceptional service, reliable products, and a customer-centric approach.

For over two decade, Niharika Scientific Center has been a leading supplier of laboratory equipment, chemicals, and educational accessories across Nepal.
